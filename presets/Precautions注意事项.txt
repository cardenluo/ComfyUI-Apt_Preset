注意事项：
文件夹外面要预留一个预设，与其他文件夹里面的同名，才能触发子文件夹预览

比如:
ckpt-sd.toml 

文件夹ckpt-basic下面还有一个
ckpt-sd.toml 


Precautions:
A preset needs to be reserved outside the folder with the same name as that in other folders to trigger the sub - folder preview.

For example:
ckpt - sd.toml

There is also a ckpt - sd.toml in the folder ckpt - basic.
